Accreditation and Accolade!

LGMVIP is one of the success stories in World's higher education. Since our foundation in 1963, we have powered our way to a consistently high ranking in the World and are one of just six post-war universities to have appeared in the world top 100.


LGMVIP is placed at the top of the Russell Group universities included in the National Student Survey (NSS) for academic support and learning opportunities.


Our score of 85.39% for overall satisfaction also sets Howgart above the national average of 82.65% in World universities.


For over 50 years, our outstanding educators have achieved a long list of accolades and our world-class researchers  are recognised internationally for their contribution to many fields of inquiry.  Our remarkable students continue to impress with stories of success within the region and on the national and world stage.


The LGMVIP continues to build its global reputation for being one of the world's most prestigious universities.
